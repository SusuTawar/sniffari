# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Pet owners looking for platonic, local playdates for their pets. They're people who treat their pets as family — they want socialization, exercise, and community for their animal companions, not for themselves. Primary context: mobile, on-the-go, casual evening/weekend browsing. Primary task: find another compatible pet nearby and start a conversation about meeting up.

## Product Purpose

Sniffari connects local pets for platonic playdates. It's a mobile-first swipe-to-meet app where pets are the only profiles, species compatibility matters, and the goal is real-world walks, play sessions, and pet friendships — not human dating. Success is measured by matches formed and messages exchanged leading to actual playdates.

## Brand Personality

Playful — Warm — Bouncy

Voice is enthusiastic but never try-hard. Tone shifts by species (whimsical for birds, sturdy for dogs, mysterious for cats, chill for reptiles) but always friendly. Colors are sunset orange-pink. Corners are rounded. Motion is bouncy with exponential easing. The app should feel like a golden-hour walk in the park — warm, light, open.

## Anti-references

- **Generic SaaS dashboards**: dry, corporate, gray-on-white utility. Sniffari is not a tool; it's a playful community.
- Absence of personality. No sterile card grids, no muted corporate blues, no heavy data tables.

## Design Principles

1. **Pet-first, not people-first** — Every pixel serves the pet's personality. No human dating tropes. No "hot or not." Every pet is a good one.
2. **Playful conviction** — Joyful without being childish. Rounded corners, bouncy motion, felt buttons with real affordance. Not a game; a tool for real playdates.
3. **Warmth without weight** — Light interface, orange-pink palette that evokes a golden-hour park. Warmth carried by color + illustration + tone, not heavy cards or ornate borders.
4. **Species-aware** — Every screen adapts to the pet in focus. Dog owners and cat owners see different copy, different puns, different affordances. No one-size-fits-all-pets template.
5. **Low-friction community** — Shortest path from "I want a playdate" to "we're chatting." Auth, location, one pet — done. No onboarding labyrinth.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG A target. Essential contrast ratios, keyboard navigation for core flows, screen-reader support on key interactions. Reduced motion respected. Full AA can be layered in post-MVP.
